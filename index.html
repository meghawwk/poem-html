HUMMING

there comes a humming bird humming

flying over the narrow stream,flowing

with the wind, the sun shining brightly

the tree-leaves rustling and whispering

clueless where to fly

backward? forward?

where to go?

what is the point?

all alone she is

couldn't even show her beautiful feathers

covered in blue,nothing but blue

is what the one who sees her says

but you could still see the light

in her pitch dark eyes you could still see

stars gleaming

hoping, for something unattainable

to reach the horizon

but still doesn't know where to go

what is the point?

all alone she is

hoping, for someone to lend her a hand

but who would?

there's no one left anymore

no more mutters, no more life

clueless she is, the hummingbird

but still humming
